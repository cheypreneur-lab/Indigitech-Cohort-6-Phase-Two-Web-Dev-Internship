About Tia Wood

Tia Wood is a Cree and Salish pop singer-songwriter from Saddle Lake Cree Nation, Canada, who gained popularity on TikTok for her "Indigenizing" sounds. She is the daughter of Earl Wood of the group Northern Cree and the sister of Juno Award-winning singer Fawn Wood. Wood signed with Sony Music Canada, released her debut EP "Pretty Red Bird" in September 2024, and received a Juno nomination for Contemporary Indigenous Artist of the Year.
